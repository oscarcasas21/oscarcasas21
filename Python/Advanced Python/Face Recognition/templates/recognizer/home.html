FamUnite

Uniting Loved Ones Through AI.

FamUnite at a Glance

FamUnite is bringing cutting-edge technology to solve one of the most heartbreaking issues happening across the globe. By using facial recognition algorithms, families can cross-reference old pictures of their loved ones with refugees and migrants that might be held in camps around the world.

What We Do

We work with partners to join families through new strides in Artificial Intelligence. We believe that new technology can make a difference when helping people in need. Specifically, we look to making sure families no longer have to be separated when crossing borders. The current disorganization in these migrant camps and lack of access to communication is not adequate. By creating this algorithm we hope to make the pain of not knowing where a loved one is, a thing of the past.

Facial Recognition Algorithm

Reinforcing our Commitment

Our organization's technology has reached an accuracy level of < 99.9%. The facial recognition system tests backend databases of photographs of individuals at migrant camps and centers.

Purpose

The End Goal

Once a match is confirmed, FamUnite will help individuals connect with their lost loved ones bycontacting our partners on the ground and will even use raised funds and donations to bring loved ones together.
